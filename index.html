History Of Cricket In India

  • 1848 - Beginnings of cricket in India

  • 1864 - Formation of the Calcutta Cricket Club

  • 1889 - First Indian cricket team to tour England

  • 1911 - Formation of the Board of Control for Cricket in India

  • 1932 - India plays its first Test match

  • 1983 - India wins the Cricket World Cup

  • 2007 - India wins the inaugural T20 World Cup

  • 2011 - India wins the Cricket World Cup for the second time

  • 2019 - India's dominance in Test cricket

  • 2024 - India wins Mens' T20 World Cup
